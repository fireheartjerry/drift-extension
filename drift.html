Privacy

Your data stays yours.

The privacy section is long because "we don't track you" apparently needs documentation now. Here you go.

TL;DR

Drift does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. Nothing leaves your browser.

Last updated: February 7, 2026

01 What Drift does Core functionality explained

Drift automatically opens a scrollable feed in a separate window while AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) generate responses. When generation ends, Drift notifies you so you can return to your conversation.

02 Data we collect Spoiler: absolutely nothing

None. Drift does not collect personal information, browsing history, keystrokes, form data, authentication tokens, or any other user data.

03 Data stored locally What lives on your machine

Drift stores the following data locally using chrome.storage:

  • User preferences: window size, position, trigger delay, focus behavior, and display settings.
  • Calibration data: saved position and dimensions of the drift window.
  • Session state: temporary flags for onboarding and whether a drift window is open.
  • Usage counter: local count of drift openings (only for UI prompts).
04 Permissions explained Why we ask, what we do
  • storage: save settings locally.
  • tabs: detect which AI tab is active.
  • windows: create/position/close the drift window.
  • alarms: periodic cleanup for orphaned windows after service worker restarts.
  • Host access to AI sites: inject content scripts to detect generation start/end.
  • Host access to Instagram: inject a small calibration overlay (if chosen).

FAQ

Stuff people ask anyway

Mostly because nobody trusts anything on the internet. Understandable.

Q Is this "a distraction app"? The opposite, actually

No. It's a waiting app. Drift only shows up when you're already idle, and it pulls you back when the response is done. The goal is fewer accidental 20-minute spirals.

Q Will it interrupt short answers? Nope — smart delay handles it

You set a trigger delay. If the model answers quickly, Drift never opens.

Q Does Drift read my chats? Zero access to your content

The extension detects the presence of generation states on supported sites. It does not store or transmit your conversation content.

Q Can I choose the feed? Your browser, your rules

Yes. Anything that can be opened in a browser window is fair game.
